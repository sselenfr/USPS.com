Epic Details

Expand each epic to review its full focus, rationale, and example user story.

Focus

This epic targets high-usage interactive tools and critical user journeys that currently have a higher Level of Effort (LOE) to Maintain or significantly impact user satisfaction. By streamlining their design, functionality, and integration, the goal is to reduce the need for customer support and decrease ongoing maintenance costs.

Rationale

Pages like "USPS Tracking – Package Tracking Page" and "USPS Tracking – Enter Tracking Number Form" have the highest views and active users. "Schedule a Pickup" and "Schedule an Appointment" are classified as High LOE. Optimizing these tools (Epic 2.1 and Epic 2.2) directly addresses operational efficiency and user satisfaction.

Example User Story
As a USPS customer, I want a streamlined process for scheduling or modifying a package pickup so that I can arrange for mail collection conveniently, reducing potential errors and support calls.

Focus

This epic aims to significantly reduce the effort and cost associated with updating and maintaining informational content across the site. It also improves user experience by ensuring content is clear, concise, and easy to find, reducing confusion and support burden.

Rationale

The audit found that roughly 68% of audited pages are content-focused, and approximately 79% of page maintenance is classified as "Low" LOE. However, pages can be "very dense and text heavy" or "link heavy". Structuring content in a journey-centric way (Epic 1.1 and Epic 1.3) will lower routine update time and enhance content discoverability.

Example User Story
As a content manager, I want an efficient process for updating product information and images (e.g., stamps, shipping supplies) so that new items and seasonal changes are reflected quickly on the Postal Store, specifically addressing the "High" LOE for "Buy Stamps."

Focus

Reduce technical debt and future rework costs by establishing a consistent, modern design across all USPS.com pages. Simultaneously, ensure compliance with Section 508 accessibility standards to serve all users equitably and mitigate legal risks.

Rationale

Several pages do not fully align with the USPS brand style guide or have outdated designs, requiring fragmented maintenance and impacting brand perception. The audit explicitly notes multiple pages lack clear accessibility for vision-impaired users. Implementing Epic 5.1 (UI Modernization) and Epic 4.2 (508 Compliance) standardizes the visual language and ensures equitable access.

Example User Story
As a USPS.com user, I want a consistent and modern look and feel across all pages so that the website appears professional and easy to use, addressing outdated designs.

Focus

Improve user experience by making it easier and faster for customers to find information and services, thereby reducing frustration and potential support inquiries. Cost reduction comes from decreased support load and more efficient self-service pathways.

Rationale

Audit findings note that "search features could use improvement to yield more relevant results" and that many pages are "link heavy which may be confusing to users navigating the site." The FAQ page search feature was specifically noted as "not strong." Improvements (Epic 5.3) directly reduce the time users spend searching and lead to a better overall experience.

Example User Story
As a user seeking help, I want the FAQ search feature to be robust and provide accurate, relevant results to my queries so that I can quickly find specific information or services.

Focus

Streamline complex transactional processes for both retail (e-commerce) and business customers, aiming to reduce operational costs through improved self-service completion rates and decreased manual intervention. User experience is enhanced through clearer, more efficient workflows.

Rationale

"The Postal Store – Home" and "Buy Stamps" are identified as High LOE to maintain due to frequent product updates. Business tools like the "Business Postage Price Calculator" are not brand style compliant and could be integrated with live data to prevent manual updates. Optimizing these modules (Epic 2.3) reduces errors, increases self-service completion, and lowers back-end support burden.

Example User Story
As a business user, I want the "Verify Postage" page to have live, real-time pricing and sizing information for accurate calculations, rather than relying on manual updates or separate tools, which would reduce manual effort for content teams and improve accuracy for users.
