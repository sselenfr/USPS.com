Help & Support

What can we help you with?

Popular: Track Package Hold Mail Change Address File a Claim Passport Application PO Box Rental Missing Mail

Where is my package?

Enter your tracking number to see real-time updates, estimated delivery, and carrier scan events.

Track a package

How do I hold my mail?

Schedule a mail hold online for 3 to 30 days. Your carrier will hold your mail until you return.

Schedule Hold Mail

How do I change my address?

Submit a Change of Address form online. A $1.10 identity verification fee applies.

Change my address

My package shows delivered but I did not receive it.

Check with neighbors and your building. If still missing, file a missing mail search request.

File a search request

How do I file a claim for a lost or damaged package?

File online within the applicable timeframe for your mail class. You will need purchase receipts and packaging info.

File a claim

How do I schedule a passport appointment?

Book an in-person appointment at a participating Post Office location. Bring required documents.

Schedule passport appointment
